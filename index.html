Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 29, 2026 | Last Updated: May 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to LLM Studio — Premium AI Chat Assistant ("LLM Studio", "we", "our", or "us"). We are dedicated to maintaining the trust and confidence of all users of our mobile application. This Privacy Policy details how we handle the collection, use, protection, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

Our commitment is simple: we respect your privacy and process data in a fully transparent, secure, and compliant manner, in line with Google Play Developer Policies and global data privacy standards.

2. Information Collection and Use

When you use LLM Studio, we strive to collect the minimum amount of personal data necessary to provide a high-quality AI chat experience. The types of data we interact with are categorized below:

  • Local Chat Data: Your prompts and assistant responses are processed and stored directly on your mobile device. We do not store your chat logs on our own centralized servers.
  • Device Information: We may collect non-identifiable technical data such as your device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers to optimize application performance.
  • User-Provided Content: Any images or audio clips you select or capture within the app are sent strictly to the underlying AI model provider for the sole purpose of generating responses (e.g., image-to-text or voice-to-text conversion). These inputs are not stored or harvested by us.

3. Device Permissions

To deliver advanced interactive AI features, LLM Studio requests explicit access to certain device features. These permissions are only requested when you initiate the relevant feature within the app, and you can revoke them at any time in your device settings:

  • Camera Permission: Required when you choose to take a photo directly through the app to send to the AI vision assistant.
  • Microphone Permission: Required for the voice input and speech-to-text feature, allowing you to speak your prompts.
  • Storage / Media Library: Required to select existing images or files to upload for AI processing, or to save generated assets and exports locally to your device.
  • Network Access: Necessary to communicate with the secure servers processing the AI models.

4. Data Usage

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Any data accessed or processed through the app is used exclusively for:

  • Operating, maintaining, and improving the app's features and overall user experience.
  • Fulfilling your requests within the application (e.g. generating AI-based text, processing voice commands, analyzing images).
  • Diagnosing technical issues, resolving bugs, and monitoring application performance.

5. Data Storage and Security

The security of your personal data is paramount. We implement robust, industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • On-Device Encryption: Chat histories, local settings, and customized parameters are stored securely on your device using encrypted, high-performance local key-value stores.
  • Transit Security: All data transmitted between the app and external AI APIs is encrypted in transit using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) / Transport Layer Security (TLS) protocols.
  • No Centralized Logging: We do not log or store the text of your conversations on our databases. All interaction history is under your control on your physical device.

6. Third-Party Services

To process your queries, the app connects to third-party AI APIs (such as Google Gemini, OpenAI, or other integrated model providers). These third-party services process the data you send (e.g. text prompts, voice clips, or images) to generate the corresponding responses.

These partners are committed to high security and privacy standards. They do not use your inputs to train public models unless specified, and their data processing is subject to their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies to understand how they handle your data.

7. Your Rights and Data Control

We respect your control over your data. Because all conversation data is stored locally, you have the absolute right to control it:

  • Data Deletion: You can completely erase your entire chat history and reset the application settings at any time directly through the Settings screen in the app.
  • App Uninstall: Uninstalling the LLM Studio application will automatically delete all locally stored data, chat records, and app configurations from your device.
  • Permission Management: You can enable or disable permissions (Camera, Microphone, Storage) at any time via your device's system settings.

8. Children's Privacy

LLM Studio is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or request personal identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete it from our systems. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can perform necessary actions.
